Balance Performance Components Across Vision Conditions

Radar Chart of Best Test Components

Balance Scale: 0 = Unable to Perform, 4 = Normal Performance

This radar chart illustrates how different vision conditions affect various components of balance. Each axis represents a different balance task from the Best test, and the chart reveals which aspects of balance are most impacted by vision changes.

Areas that show greater differences between vision conditions indicate balance components that are more dependent on visual input.
