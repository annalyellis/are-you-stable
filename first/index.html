Age Distribution and Falls Risk Relationship

Analysis of Falls Efficacy Scale (FES) Scores by Age Group

This visualization shows the relationship between age groups and falls risk as measured by the Falls Efficacy Scale (FES). Higher scores indicate greater concern about falling. The visualization helps identify which age groups have higher falls risk perception.

The number above each bar (n) indicates the sample size for each age group.
