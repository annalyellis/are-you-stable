Relationship Between BMI, Falls History, and Balance Performance

A Multivariate Analysis of Falls Risk Factors

This scatter plot explores the relationship between BMI and overall balance performance scores. The size of each point represents the number of falls in the past 12 months, while the color represents gender.

This visualization helps identify potential risk patterns and how these variables interact to influence falls risk.
