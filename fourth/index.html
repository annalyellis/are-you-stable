Falls Risk by Gender and Age Group

Comparison of Falls Efficacy Scale (FES) Scores

This grouped bar chart compares falls risk (as measured by the Falls Efficacy Scale) between genders across different age groups. The chart reveals how gender may influence falls risk perception at different life stages.

Higher FES scores indicate greater concern about falling during daily activities. Sample sizes for each group are displayed above the bars.
