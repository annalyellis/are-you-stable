Balance and Falls Risk Visualizations

Exploring factors that affect balance performance and falls risk in different populations

Welcome to the Home Page

We have created 5 static visualizations as part of the exploratory data analysis process. Using these, we will come up interesting, interactive ways to visualize the data. We have plans to make it interactive in that one could test their own balance. We may involve a camera or some sort of test where the viewer is able to take a test and see how different aspects of their balance compare to others. In addition to this, we will also make visualizations that convey informations about the trends present in the data. Through both of these types of visualizations, the viewer should be able to learn about both their own stability and human balancing capabilites in general.
