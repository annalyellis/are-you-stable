Balance and Falls Risk Visualizations

Exploring factors that affect balance performance and falls risk in different populations

Project Overview

This project provides an interactive exploration of the relationships between various factors affecting balance and falls risk. Using data from the BDSinfo dataset, we visualize how age, gender, BMI, vision conditions, and other variables interact to influence balance performance and falls risk.

These visualizations aim to provide insights that could help in understanding falls risk factors and potentially guide interventions to reduce falls in vulnerable populations.
