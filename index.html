Balance and Falls Risk Visualizations

Exploring factors that affect balance performance and falls risk in different populations

Write-up

So far we have a quiz that allows you to discover how your balance compares to others. This quiz asks you varying questions related to the datasets, including your age, how you perform on everyday activities, and the amount of exercise you do. At the end of the quiz you are told how good your balance is and there is a visualization that shows you how you compare to others. Then, you can explore multiple other visualizaitons that regard the larger trends in how balance relates to other factors including age, gender, and more. The most challenging part of the project will be ensuring that the information we display is meaningful to the viewer. There are many visualizations one could make that would display the data in our dataset, and we have already made several. However, it is difficult but important to make compelling visualizations that the viewer either finds relevant to their own life or innately fascinating. This might also involve using data from outside our dataset, which could complicate things.
